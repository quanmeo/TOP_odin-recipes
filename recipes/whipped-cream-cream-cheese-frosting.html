Whipped Cream Cream Cheese Frosting

Description

This whipped cream cheese frosting is so decadent everyone will be asking if they can lick the bowl! It pipes very well.

Ingredients

  • heavy whipping cream
  • cream cheese
  • white sugar
  • salt
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat whipping cream in a mixing bowl until stiff peaks form; set aside.
  2. Combine cream cheese, sugar, salt, and vanilla in a large bowl. Beat until smooth, then fold in whipped cream.
